step 1. plug in your MIDI device.
step 2. start 'keyBordL.exe'.
step 3. press '1' in order to enter 'fast setup'.
step 4. select your MIDI device.
step 5. press all MIDI keys/button you want to register.
step 6. press any key to save and continue.
step 7. press '4' in order to enter 'run'.

complete! 

note 1: 
don't press any MIDI keys or keyboard key while the application is active, 
because this will cause the application to exit 'run' mode.

note 2: 
if something doesn't work, try to restart the application.
